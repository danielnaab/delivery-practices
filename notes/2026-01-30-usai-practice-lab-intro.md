## Facilitation Flow

| Phase | Duration | Instructions |
|-------|----------|--------------|
| **Presentation** | 10 min | Walk through slides, demo a documented practice |
| **Silent brainstorm** | 5 min | Everyone adds items to all three columns. No talking. |
| **Pair share** | 5 min | Find a partner, discuss your items, add new ones sparked by conversation |
| **Clustering** | 10 min | As a group, drag related items together, name the clusters |
| **Dot vote** | 5 min | Everyone gets 3 dots, vote on most important themes |
| **Discussion** | 5 min | Discuss top-voted themes, what they tell us |
| **Commit** | 5 min | Agree on first Practice Lab date, next steps |
