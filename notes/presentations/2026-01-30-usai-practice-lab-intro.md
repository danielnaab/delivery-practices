# The Opportunity

**Teams with documented, evolving practices:**

- Onboard new members faster
- Maintain consistency across the codebase
- Learn from what works (and what doesn't)
- Demonstrate engineering leadership to clients

**Shared practices = force multiplier for the whole team**
